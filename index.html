Crystal Collector

Instructions:

  • At the beginning of each game, a random target number is generated.
  • The goal of the game is to make your score exactly equal to the target number.
  • You can increase your score by clicking on any of the 4 crystals.
  • Each crystal will increase your score by a different amount.
  • The amount by which each crystal increases your score is randomly generated at the start of each game.
  • If your score exceeds the target number, you will lose.
